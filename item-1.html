Artips

School Project // Innovation award // Mobile App + Hardware Sensor

The project

Although we always work with hybrid technology, Artips was the exception. The client came to us with a native codebase (iOS and Android) and asked us to interface a webview with his app. When you open the app, the content you see is in a webview. But when you tap a 'special edition', it opens a native view.

The stack

Along with the usual Ionic + Angular we always use, we took advantage of the cordova payment plugin [LINK]. We also made our own plugin to interface with the native views. As for the back, our needs were very simple, so we used Firebase.
